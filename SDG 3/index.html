About This Mission

Good health is essential for sustainable development. This goal focuses on reducing mortality, improving healthcare systems, supporting mental health, and ensuring access to essential services for people of all ages.

Key Goals

Universal Health Coverage

Access to quality health services without financial hardship.

Reduce Child Mortality

Preventable deaths of newborns and children must be eliminated.

Support Mental Health

Promote emotional well-being and access to mental health care.

End Epidemics

Combat diseases like AIDS, HIV, and tuberculosis.
